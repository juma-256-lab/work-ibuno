Who We Are

Ibuno Design SMC Limited - Mpenzi Paper Studio is a legally registered corporate entity in Uganda. We are recorded among employers with unallocated contributions at the National Social Security Fund (NSSF) Uganda, confirming compliance with national labor and social security regulations.


We operate physically at 250A Waligo Road, Kampala. Our work spans architectural illustrations, custom event designs, paper products, and business process solutions.
